Who we are & What we do?

PT. Andalira Cipta Abadi is a distribution and industrial solutions company established on December 2, 2002, and has been trusted as an Authorized Distributor of PT. 3M Indonesia since 2003, with the support of a network of head offices, branches, and operational facilities spread across various strategic regions in Indonesia. As a reliable industrial partner, we are here to provide products and solutions based on Industrial Tapes, Adhesives, Abrasives, as well as supporting manufacturing services that meet international quality standards.

We are a team of professionals with integrated technical and commercial expertise, consistently delivering end-to-end industrial solutions from technical consultation, production, to distribution, to support the needs of various industrial sectors and help customers achieve efficiency and sustainable business performance.
