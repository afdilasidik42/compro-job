Short About Us

Looking for reliable industrial solutions?

PT. Andalira Cipta Abadi has been a trusted Authorized Distributor of 3M Indonesia since 2003, specializing in industrial tapes, adhesives, and abrasive products.

Starting from a small shop in North Jakarta, we have grown into a company with a 1,600 m² head office located in the Jababeka Industrial Area, Cikarang, supported by branch offices in major cities across Java.

  • Authorized Distributor of 3M Indonesia
  • Strong sales & technical support team
  • Fast and reliable customer service
  • Nationwide distribution coverage
